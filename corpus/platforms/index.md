# Standards of proficiency for registered nurses

The seven platforms (NMC, 2018):

- [Platform 1: Being an accountable professional](platform-1.md)
- [Platform 2: Promoting health and preventing ill health](platform-2.md)
- [Platform 3: Assessing needs and planning care](platform-3.md)
- [Platform 4: Providing and evaluating care](platform-4.md)
- [Platform 5: Leading and managing nursing care and working in teams](platform-5.md)
- [Platform 6: Improving safety and quality of care](platform-6.md)
- [Platform 7: Coordinating care](platform-7.md)

Plus **Annexe A** (communication and relationship management skills) and **Annexe B** (nursing procedures).

Achievement is supervised and assessed under the [SSSA](../sssa/index.md). Every platform enacts sections of [the Code](../code/index.md). Back to the [explorer root](../index.md).
