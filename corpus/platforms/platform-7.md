# Platform 7: Coordinating care

At the point of registration, the registered nurse will be able to:

- **7.1** Understand and apply the principles of partnership, collaboration and interagency working across all relevant sectors
- **7.2** Understand health legislation and current health and social care policies, and the mechanisms involved in influencing policy development and change, appreciating the impact of policy on practice
- **7.3** Understand and apply the principles of human factors, environmental factors and systems failures to identify and reduce the risk of clinical error
- **7.4** Prioritise the needs of groups of people and manage resources effectively and safely
- **7.5** Understand and apply the principles of delivering care across multiple agencies and when transferring care between different settings
- **7.6** Demonstrate the ability to effectively coordinate care in complex or emergency nursing situations
- **7.7** Demonstrate understanding and apply the principles of health informatics and digital technology to deliver and coordinate care and to understand the needs of people
- **7.8** Understand the principles of health economics, including the role of clinical effectiveness, efficiency and the ethical allocation of resources
- **7.9** Work collaboratively with all members of the multidisciplinary team, and across all settings, to support the coordinated delivery of person-centred care
- **7.10** Understand the challenge of providing effective nursing care for people with complex co-morbidities and respond to this by coordinating care effectively
- **7.11** Demonstrate the ability to identify and manage risks and concerns in relation to the care of people across all care settings
- **7.12** Demonstrate knowledge of the roles of other health and care professionals and the contribution they make to providing coordinated care across all settings
- **7.13** Demonstrate an understanding of the complexities of providing mental health, learning disability and children's nursing care in a range of integrated settings
