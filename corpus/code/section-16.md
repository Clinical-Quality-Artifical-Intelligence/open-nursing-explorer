# 16. Act without delay if you believe there is a risk to patient safety or public protection

Theme: [Preserve Safety](theme-3-preserve-safety.md)

## To achieve this, you must:

- **16.1** Raise and escalate concerns about patient or public safety
- **16.2** Raise concerns immediately if asked to practise beyond your role
- **16.3** Tell someone in authority if you experience problems preventing you from working within the Code
- **16.4** Acknowledge and act on all concerns raised to you
- **16.5** Not obstruct anyone who wants to raise a concern
- **16.6** Protect anyone you manage from harm after a concern is raised
