# 3. Make sure that people's physical, social and psychological needs are assessed and responded to

Theme: [Prioritise People](theme-1-prioritise-people.md)

## To achieve this, you must:

- **3.1** Pay special attention to promoting wellbeing, preventing ill health and meeting changing needs during all life stages
- **3.2** Recognise and respond compassionately to the needs of those in the last few days and hours of life
- **3.3** Act in partnership with those receiving care, helping them access relevant health and social care
- **3.4** Act as an advocate for the vulnerable, challenging poor practice and discriminatory attitudes
