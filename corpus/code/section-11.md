# 11. Be accountable for your decisions to delegate tasks and duties to other people

Theme: [Practise Effectively](theme-2-practise-effectively.md)

## To achieve this, you must:

- **11.1** Only delegate tasks within the other person's scope of competence
- **11.2** Make sure everyone you delegate to is adequately supervised and supported
- **11.3** Confirm that the outcome of any delegated task meets the required standard
