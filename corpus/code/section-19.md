# 19. Be aware of, and reduce as far as possible, any potential for harm associated with your practice

Theme: [Preserve Safety](theme-3-preserve-safety.md)

## To achieve this, you must:

- **19.1** Take measures to reduce the likelihood of mistakes, near misses, harm
- **19.2** Take account of current evidence in reducing mistakes and the impact of human factors
- **19.3** Keep to recommended practice in relation to infection prevention and control
- **19.4** Take all reasonable personal precautions to avoid health risks
