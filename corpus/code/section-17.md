# 17. Raise concerns immediately if you believe a person is vulnerable or at risk

Theme: [Preserve Safety](theme-3-preserve-safety.md)

## To achieve this, you must:

- **17.1** Take all reasonable steps to protect vulnerable people from harm, neglect or abuse
- **17.2** Share information if you believe someone may be at risk of harm
- **17.3** Have knowledge of and keep to the relevant laws about protecting vulnerable people
