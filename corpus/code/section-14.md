# 14. Be open and candid with all service users about all aspects of care and treatment

Theme: [Preserve Safety](theme-3-preserve-safety.md)

## To achieve this, you must:

- **14.1** Act immediately to put right the situation if someone has suffered actual harm
- **14.2** Explain fully and promptly what has happened and apologise
- **14.3** Document all events formally and escalate if appropriate
