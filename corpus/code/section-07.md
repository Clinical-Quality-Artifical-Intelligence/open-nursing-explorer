# 7. Communicate clearly

Theme: [Practise Effectively](theme-2-practise-effectively.md)

## To achieve this, you must:

- **7.1** Use terms that people can understand
- **7.2** Take reasonable steps to meet people's language and communication needs
- **7.3** Use a range of verbal and non-verbal communication methods
- **7.4** Check people's understanding from time to time
- **7.5** Be able to communicate clearly and effectively in English
