# 23. Cooperate with all investigations and audits

Theme: [Promote Professionalism and Trust](theme-4-promote-professionalism-and-trust.md)

## To achieve this, you must:

- **23.1** Cooperate with audits of training records and registration records
- **23.2** Tell the NMC and employers about any caution or charge against you
- **23.3** Tell employers if your practice has been restricted
- **23.4** Tell the NMC and employers if disciplined by any regulatory body
- **23.5** Give your NMC Pin when reasonably requested
