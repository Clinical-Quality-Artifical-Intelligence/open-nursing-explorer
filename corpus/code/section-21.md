# 21. Uphold your position as a registered nurse, midwife or nursing associate

Theme: [Promote Professionalism and Trust](theme-4-promote-professionalism-and-trust.md)

## To achieve this, you must:

- **21.1** Refuse all but the most trivial gifts, favours or hospitality
- **21.2** Never ask for or accept loans from anyone in your care
- **21.3** Act with honesty and integrity in any financial dealings
- **21.4** Make sure advertisements are accurate, responsible, ethical, not misleading
- **21.5** Never use your status to promote causes not related to health
- **21.6** Cooperate with the media only when appropriate
