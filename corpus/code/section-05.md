# 5. Respect people's right to privacy and confidentiality

Theme: [Prioritise People](theme-1-prioritise-people.md)

## To achieve this, you must:

- **5.1** Respect a person's right to privacy in all aspects of their care
- **5.2** Make sure people are informed about how and why information is used and shared
- **5.3** Respect that a person's right to privacy and confidentiality continues after they have died
- **5.4** Share necessary information with other professionals when patient safety and public protection override confidentiality
- **5.5** Share information sensitively and in a way people can understand
